# Coffee-Sales-Dashboard

1. **Project Description**:
A comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze coffee shop sales performance, customer buying behavior, store profitability, and product trends across three locations (Hell's Kitchen, Astoria, and Lower Manhattan)
**This dashboard provides deep insights into:**
  Total sales performance
  Product-wise sales contribution
  Store-level profitability
  Peak business hours
  Sales vs transactions analysis
  Best-selling coffee products

2. **Key Insights**
  Hell’s Kitchen generated the highest sales among all stores.
  Sales distribution across stores is relatively balanced.
  The business offers a wide product range with 80 unique products.
  Coffee-based beverages dominate overall sales.
  Espresso products generate the highest revenue.
  Tea products also contribute significantly to total sales.
  Monday is the most profitable day.
  Wednesday and Thursday are the busiest days.
  Peak store traffic occurs between 7 AM and 10 AM.
  Morning hours contribute the highest sales volume. Sales gradually decline after noon. Evening traffic is comparatively low.

4. **Recommendations**
----Sales Optimization-----
Introduce loyalty programs for frequent customers.
Bundle breakfast and coffee combos.
-----Inventory Planning----
Maintain a higher stock for espresso products.
Forecast demand based on weekday trends.
-------Staffing---------
Increase employee allocation during morning rush hours.
-------Marketing--------
Promote low-performing products using combo offers.
Run Monday-specific promotional campaigns.
